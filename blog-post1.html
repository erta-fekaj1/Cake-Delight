5 Tips for Perfect Cakes

1. Measure Ingredients Precisely

One of the biggest mistakes bakers make is inaccurate measuring. Use a kitchen scale for precise measurements, especially for dry ingredients like flour.

2. Use Room Temperature Ingredients

Ensure ingredients like eggs, butter, and milk are at room temperature. This helps create a smoother batter, leading to a more even bake.

3. Don’t Overmix the Batter

Mixing too much can result in dense, tough cakes. Mix until ingredients are just combined to keep your cakes light and fluffy.

4. Preheat Your Oven

Always preheat your oven before placing your cake inside. This ensures even cooking and prevents underbaked centers or overbrowned edges.

5. Test for Doneness

Check if your cake is done by inserting a toothpick into the center. If it comes out clean, your cake is ready. Avoid overbaking, which dries out the cake.
