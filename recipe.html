Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Preparation Time

Approximately 1 hour

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9-inch round baking pan.
  3. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add water, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and vinegar. Mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cake to cool for 10 minutes before removing from the pan.
  8. Frost as desired and serve.
