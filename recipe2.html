Red Velvet Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp white distilled vinegar

Preparation Time

Approximately 1 hour and 20 minutes

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vanilla, and vinegar.
  5. Mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined and a smooth batter is formed.
  6. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared baking pans.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Frost as desired and serve.
