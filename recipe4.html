Carrot Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Preparation Time

Approximately 1 hour 30 minutes

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  4. In another bowl, beat the eggs, sugar, and vegetable oil until smooth.
  5. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until combined.
  6. Fold in the grated carrots and nuts (if using).
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Allow the cake to cool completely in the pan.
  10. Frost with cream cheese frosting and serve.
