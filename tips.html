Baking Tips & Tricks

Use a kitchen scale for precise measurements. Baking is a science, and small errors can make a big difference.

Ensure butter, eggs, and milk are at room temperature to create a smooth batter and ensure even baking.

Overmixing can cause your cake to be dense and tough. Mix just until the ingredients are combined.
